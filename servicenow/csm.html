Core Modules

Omnichannel. Proactive. Intelligent.

ServiceNow CSM connects front-office and back-office operations so your agents always have context, and your customers always get answers.

Case Management
Unify all customer interactions — email, chat, phone, portal, social — in a single case. Agents see the full customer history and context at a glance.
Agent Workspace
A purpose-built agent UI with AI-generated response suggestions, guided decisions, and real-time SLA tracking. Agents handle more cases with less effort.
Proactive Customer Service
Detect issues before customers call — monitor connected products, trigger proactive outreach, and resolve problems automatically before they escalate.
Virtual Agent & AI Self-Service
Let customers resolve their own issues with an intelligent chatbot. Now Assist handles FAQs, order status, account changes, and troubleshooting 24/7.
Field Service Management
Dispatch the right technician, with the right parts, at the right time. Optimize schedules with AI and give customers real-time visibility into on-site visits.
Key Benefits

The impact our CSM customers see.

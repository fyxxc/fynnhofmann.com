AMS Capabilities

Your platform. Our responsibility.

NovaTech AMS gives you a fully dedicated, certified team that owns your ServiceNow instance — proactively and reactively — so your IT team can stay focused on strategy.

24/7 Monitoring & Alerting
Continuous monitoring of instance health, performance metrics, scheduler jobs, and integration flows. Proactive alerting before issues impact users.
Upgrade Management
End-to-end upgrade planning, execution, and post-upgrade validation for every ServiceNow release. Zero surprises, full rollback plan, and regression testing included.
Continuous Enhancements
Monthly enhancement sprints to extend and improve your instance. New modules, workflow improvements, UI updates — prioritized from your business backlog.
Break/Fix & Incident Resolution
Guaranteed SLAs for break/fix resolution. P1 critical issues resolved within 2 hours. Dedicated AMS team knows your instance inside out — no ticket queues, no ramp-up time.
Governance & Reporting
Monthly service reviews with executive dashboards, SLA reports, enhancement velocity metrics, and platform health scorecards. Full transparency at every level.

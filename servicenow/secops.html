Core Modules

Security and IT — finally working as one.

ServiceNow Security Operations bridges the gap between security teams and IT, enabling faster response, less manual work, and complete audit trails.

Security Incident Response (SIR)
Ingest alerts from SIEM tools, enrich with threat intelligence, and auto-prioritize. Orchestrate cross-team response with built-in playbooks and one-click remediation.
Vulnerability Response
Connect scanner data (Tenable, Qualys, Rapid7) to CMDB and auto-create remediation tasks. Prioritize by business risk, not just CVSS score.
Threat Intelligence
Aggregate threat feeds (STIX/TAXII, ISACs, commercial feeds) and enrich incidents automatically. Understand attacker TTPs before they reach your environment.
Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)
Map controls to frameworks (ISO 27001, SOC 2, NIST, NIS2). Automate evidence collection, track exceptions, and generate audit reports on demand.
SOAR & Automated Playbooks
Build no-code playbooks with Flow Designer. Automate containment, notification, enrichment, and closure steps — reducing analyst workload by up to 70%.
